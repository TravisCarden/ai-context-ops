# Playbook: Maintenance

**Status:** scaffold. MVP (documented commands + cadence).

- `pi update` to keep Pi, its packages, and model catalogs current.
- `brew upgrade claude-code` to keep claude-code current.
- Re-run `diagnose.md` periodically to catch regressions in savings.
